"Everything as a Service" (XaaS)

Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) paved the way for the XaaS (Everything-as-a-Service) model. Now, industries beyond technology are adopting this approach. Car manufacturers like Volvo and Porsche offer vehicle subscription services, allowing consumers to swap cars instead of buying or leasing them. Similarly, home appliance companies are offering "appliances-as-a-service," where customers pay a monthly fee to access high-end refrigerators, washing machines, and even furniture without owning them.

Circular Economy and Resale Platforms

Sustainability-driven business models are gaining traction, particularly in the fashion and consumer electronics industries. Brands like Patagonia and Levi's have introduced buy-back programs where customers return used items in exchange for store credit. These items are then refurbished and resold, reducing waste and promoting sustainability.
